  • TEKKEN World Tour and Smash.gg

    The TEKKEN World Tour (TWT) is an international Tekken tournament series organised by Bandai Namco Entertainment Inc. in partnership with Twitch. The Tour includes a "Dojo tournament system" that allows competitors to earn ranking points for the Tour leaderboard at their local events. All TWT tournament brackets are run onthe esports tournament hosting site Smash.gg. To learn more about the TWT, click here.

    This season, we would like to include Circuit qualifiers as Dojo events on the TWT and as such, players will be required to create Smash.gg accounts and sign up to qualifiers on the site when they become available (players will be promptly notified when qualifiers go up on the site).

  • To create a Smash.gg account:

    1. Click on the "Login/Register" button at the bottom of the menu. This can be found on the left side of the screen (PC) or accessed by clicking the three-line symbol on the top-left side of the screen (mobile).
    2. Once the Login/Register pop-up appears, click on the "Register" tab, fill in your details as required and follow any further instructions. (Kindly use the samedetails you have used to sign up here.)
